About

Hello! I'm a dedicated and passionate educator currently working as a Learning Specialist at EdX and a Quality Assurance Professional at DataAnnotation.tech. With a strong foundation in Data Analytics and Machine Learning, I am committed to helping students excel in these fields by bridging the gaps in their knowledge and enhancing their learning experience.


Professional Background

I have a rich background in education, specializing in tutoring students in Data Analytics and Machine Learning. My journey has been marked by a strong commitment to providing high-quality education and support. At EdX, my role involves grading student work, debugging code, and tutoring students by identifying and addressing their learning needs. I also contribute to improving the coursework based on student feedback and performance.

In my quality assurance role at DataAnnotation.tech, I engage in A/B testing of AI models to ensure optimal performance and improve training data. My work involves meticulous analysis and adjustment of model outputs to enhance overall AI learning processes.

My Approach to Problem-Solving

I approach challenges with a data-driven mindset, always looking for optimal solutions. When faced with new problems, I adapt by researching and aligning my methods with the specific needs of the situation, ensuring that students receive the most relevant and effective guidance without needing significant changes to their work.


Professional Goals

Looking ahead, I aim to transition from education into a full-time role as an Analyst or Data Scientist within the next 5-10 years. I am eager to apply my analytical skills and problem-solving abilities in new contexts, contributing to impactful data-driven decisions.


A Project That Exemplifies My Work

A notable project I led involved A/B testing the efficacy of Covid-19 lockdowns. By analyzing outbreak data from various regions before and during lockdown periods, I demonstrated significant reductions in virus spread in over 90% of the countries that implemented these measures. This project highlights my ability to use data analytics to provide clear, actionable insights into critical global issues.
